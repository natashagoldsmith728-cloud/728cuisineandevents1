About 728 Cuisine & Events

728 Cuisine & Events is a boutique catering and event brand focused on intimate dinners, celebrations, and small gatherings. Our goal is simple: combine beautiful presentation, thoughtful details, and great food so you can actually enjoy your own event.

From private dinners to milestone celebrations, we work with you to design a menu and atmosphere that match your vision. Every event is treated like it matters—because it does.

Signature Services

Private Chef Experiences

Multi-course dinners in your home or venue with a custom menu, grocery shopping, cooking, service, and cleanup included.

Catering for Small Events

Perfect for birthdays, showers, graduations, and small corporate events. Buffet, plated, or family-style options available.

Event Styling & Coordination

Black-and-gold inspired decor, timelines, and setup support to keep your event polished and stress-free from start to finish.

Our Story

728 represents more than a name—it reflects important dates, memories, and the idea that every moment is worth celebrating. After years of cooking for family, friends, and special occasions, 728 Cuisine & Events grew into a way to serve the community with the same care.

The brand leans into a black-and-gold theme to represent elegance, confidence, and celebration. Whether it’s a cozy dinner for two or a room full of guests, 728 is about making each person feel seen, welcomed, and well fed.

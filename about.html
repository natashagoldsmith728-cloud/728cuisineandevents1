About 728 Cuisine & Events

728 Cuisine & Events is a Richmond-area catering and event company that focuses on great food, smooth planning, and a luxury feel. Our black-and-gold brand reflects the quality, care, and celebration we bring to each event.

Our Story

728 started from a love of cooking for family and friends. Over time, that passion grew into a business that serves weddings, birthdays, graduations, corporate events, and more.

We believe in treating every event like it matters—because it does. We listen to your ideas, ask questions, and build a menu that fits your taste and budget.

Our Values

  • Respecting each client’s vision and culture
  • Providing clear, honest communication
  • Delivering consistent quality every time
  • Keeping events organized, on time, and stress-free

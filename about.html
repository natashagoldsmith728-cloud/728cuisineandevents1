Meet the Chef & Vision

About 728 Cuisine & Events

728 Cuisine & Events brings together elevated flavors, polished service, and a love for celebrating life’s most important moments.

Chef & Owner, Natasha Goldsmith

Natasha Goldsmith is the CEO and Owner of 728 Cuisine & Events. Since 2013, she has been curating private dinners, celebrations, and catered events that blend soulful flavor with fine-dining presentation.

With a background in business management and a passion for hospitality, Natasha understands that every event is personal. From the first inquiry to the last plated course, her focus is on creating an experience that feels seamless, thoughtful, and memorable for every guest.

Our Philosophy

728 Cuisine & Events is built on three pillars:

  • Elevated Flavor – Menus crafted with quality ingredients and bold, balanced taste.
  • Intentional Design – Tablescapes, plating, and ambiance that match your vision.
  • Effortless Experience – Professional planning and service so you can enjoy your event.

Serving Richmond & Beyond

Based in the Richmond, Virginia area, 728 Cuisine & Events supports weddings, corporate events, milestone birthdays, showers, intimate dinners, and more across Central Virginia and surrounding regions.

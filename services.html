Signature Services

From full-service catering to intimate private dinners, every offering is designed to feel elegant, personal, and stress-free.

Catering Experiences

Full-service catering for weddings, milestone birthdays, showers, corporate functions, and more. We handle menu design, staffing, and presentation so you can enjoy your event.

  • Custom menus based on your taste and theme
  • Buffet, plated, or family-style service
  • Set-up and breakdown of food service areas

Private Chef Dinners

Bring the restaurant experience home. Ideal for anniversaries, celebrations, or a special night in with guests.

  • Multi-course chef-curated menus
  • On-site cooking, plating, and service
  • Optional wine or beverage pairing guidance

Events & Celebrations

Thoughtfully designed events that combine beautiful food, décor, and flow—from the welcome to the final toast.

  • Event coordination support
  • Tablescapes and décor recommendations
  • Collaborations with venues and vendors

Custom Menus

Menus tailored to dietary needs, preferences, and the overall feel of your event—whether classic, modern, or a fusion of both.

  • Seasonal, locally-focused options
  • Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-conscious dishes
  • Brunch, lunch, dinner, and dessert tables

Investment & Booking Details

Every event is unique, so pricing is customized based on guest count, menu selections, service style, staffing needs, and travel. During our consultation, we’ll review your vision and provide a clear estimate.

To begin planning, we recommend having your date, approximate guest count, and location in mind. A retainer may be required to secure your date.
